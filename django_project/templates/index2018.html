Technovate


Technovate was only born last year and all the work put in already made it Central India's largest techno-cultural fest receiving eager and diverse participation from all over the nation and this year we seek no less. Technovate 2k17 was a joyride of cultural and technical challenges brewing fast paced, tense competiton among participants as their skills were put through the most interesting tests ranging from coding challenges, robotics, drone challenges to journalism, dance and gaming. Now it's time for Technovate 2.0.






Tech Events

Persuit of Appieness more_vert
Persuit of Appienessclose

Persuit of appieness is App development event for both iOS and Android. For a given problem statement you need to develop an app (platform you a free to choose) and after several rounds of top 5 will showcase them in Technovate.

Stages:-
  • Abstract submission
  • Presentation of internal working and flow
  • Working Demo

Rules and problem statement will be announced 1st week of February and can be downloaded from here.

Game of Codes more_vert
Game of Codesclose

Sick of all the coding competitions where someone more experienced than you takes away the reward, at game of codes, that is not the case, join us in a competition aimed solely at school students and first year students, make sure you carry your id’s for this one.

Rules and problem statement will be announced 1st week of February and can be downloaded from here.

Machine learning & data science are now hot topic in industries. With machine learning and data science now almost everything is possible. Almost everywhere ML is applied. This competetion is about using dataset provided and do analyis and prediction.

Hack D Unhackd' more_vert
Hack D Unhackd'close

We are proud to announce a unqiue hackathon, Hack D UnhackD' where anyone can participate. Hack D UnhackD' is organized by students of IIIT-Naya Raipur. Aim of this event is to serve the development of both programming and hardware community by exploiting the potential of young and worthy developers. A well experieced to newbei both can take advantage of this platform to learn, build, and create stuff from scratch. Visit website: Hack D Unhackd'
