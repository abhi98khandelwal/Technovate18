Technovate


Technovate was only born last year and all the work put in already made it Central India's largest techno-cultural fest receiving eager and diverse participation from all over the nation and this year we seek no less. Technovate 2k17 was a joyride of cultural and technical challenges brewing fast paced, tense competiton among participants as their skills were put through the most interesting tests ranging from coding challenges, robotics, drone challenges to journalism, dance and gaming. Now it's time for Technovate 2.0

Game of Codes

Sick of all the coding competitions where someone more experienced than you takes away the reward, at game of codes, that is not the case, join us in a competition aimed solely at school students and first year students, make sure you carry your id’s for this one.

Backyard Datascience
Download herearchive
-->

A two day long contest, comprising online round before the fest and offline rounds in two phases - Spot Round and Final Round. With a given dataset, a team has to apply algorithms and try to find the accuracy for the given target parameters. The dataset will be provided at the time of contest, and the team size can range from 1 to 3 (maximum). Languages supported are R or Python. Libraries can be used, but at the time of presentation, judges can ask the questions related to the used libraries. The Backyard Data Science event is one of it's own kind, among one of the unique ones in India to be held. There will be invited talks by the esteemed panel of judges.

A well experieced to newbei both can take advantage of this platform to learn, build, and create stuff from scratch. Visit website: Hack D Unhackd'
